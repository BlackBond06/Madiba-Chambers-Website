Family-Based Immigration

One of the primary objectives of the immigration lawyers at our law firm is to join people with their relatives on a permanent or semi-permanent basis. Each immigration attorney in our law firm’s immigration practice group practices family-based immigration and naturalization law and has found great success helping immigrants from all over the world.

While some immigration law firms limit their service to report processing and guidance, we comprehend the pain of family separation, and provide individual service in a supportive setting.

Employment-Based Immigration

In Nigera, approximately 6,000 immigrant visas are released for employment-based categories every year. As anyone seeking such a visa might imagine, the qualifications and paperwork required for these visas can differ significantly, and the process could be quite intricate and involve many types of paperwork

This is the gap that the immigration law unit of Madiba Chambers aim to fill by its result-oriented and personalized advisory and consultancy immigration services.

Naturalization & Citizenship

Have you been living in Nigeria as a permanent resident and think it is time to secure citizenship? The citizenship attorneys at Madiba Chambers help people meet their naturalization and citizenship requirements.
Our citizenship and naturalization lawyers can take care of the most intricate cases, including conquering obstacles triggered by illegal residency. If you are ready and willing to pursue naturalization or citizenship, our immigration lawyers are prepared to help. The citizenship attorneys at our law firm offer efficient, economical services that are focused on outcomes.
